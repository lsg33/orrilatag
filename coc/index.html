Code of Conduct

Respectful Behavior

  • Bigotry: Nova Tag is committed to providing a welcoming environment for all players. Any form of bigotry, including but not limited to racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, or any other discriminatory behavior, is strictly prohibited.
  • Harassment: Harassment of any kind, including unwanted comments or behavior that targets an individual’s personal characteristics or identity, is not allowed.

Fair Play

  • Hacking and Exploiting: Using cheats, hacks, or exploiting bugs to gain an unfair advantage in the game is strictly prohibited. This includes any software or methods that modify the game or provide unauthorized benefits.
  • Out of Bounds: Do not attempt to circumvent the game’s boundaries or explore areas not intended for player interaction. Engaging in such activities may disrupt gameplay for others and is against the rules.

Appropriate Content

  • Inappropriate Content: Do not share or display any content that is offensive, explicit, or inappropriate. This includes language, images, or messages that are deemed unsuitable for the community.

Communication

  • Spam and Unsolicited Messages: Avoid spamming the chat or sending unsolicited messages to other players. Communication should be respectful and relevant to the game.

Account Security

  • Account Protection: Keep your account information secure. Do not share your password or account details with anyone. You are responsible for all activity that occurs under your account.

Bullying and Abuse

  • Bullying: Bullying or targeting other players with the intent to cause distress or harm is not tolerated. This includes verbal abuse, intimidation, or any form of persistent negative behavior.
  • Reporting Abuse: Report any instances of bullying or abuse to the game’s support team or moderators for investigation.

Reporting Issues

  • Submit Reports: If you encounter any violations of this Code of Conduct or issues within the game, please report them to the appropriate moderators or support team.

Compliance

  • Adherence to Rules: Follow any additional rules or guidelines provided by the game developers or moderators. Compliance with these rules ensures a positive experience for everyone.

Consequences

  • Penalties: Failure to adhere to this Code of Conduct may result in penalties, including temporary or permanent suspension of your account, depending on the severity of the violation.
