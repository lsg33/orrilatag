Our Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

1. Gameplay Data: We collect data generated during your gameplay, including in-game actions, scores, and interactions with other players.

2. Device Information: We gather information about your device, such as device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

3. Analytics: We use third-party analytics tools to gather usage and performance data to improve the Game.

How We Use Your Information

1. Gameplay Enhancement: We use your data to enhance and optimize your gameplay experience, including improving game mechanics and balancing.

2. Analytics: Your information helps us analyze player behavior and preferences to improve the Game’s features and performance.

Data Sharing

1. Authorized Third Parties: We may share gameplay and analytics data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in optimizing and enhancing the Game. They are obligated to protect your information.

2. Legal Compliance: We may disclose your data to comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or respond to legal requests.

Your Choices

1. Opt-out: You can choose not to share gameplay data by adjusting your in-game privacy settings or ceasing to use the Game. If you wish to have your information removed, send an email to support@novatag.lol. For a faster response, visit the Request Deletion section of the website and fill out the form with your details and deletion request.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Children’s Privacy

Nova Tag is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The updated version will be available within the Game, and you should review it periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at support@novatag.lol.
